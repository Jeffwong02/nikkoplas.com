Our Heritage & Commitment

Established Excellence in Johor Bahru

Founded in 1989, Industri Nikkoplas Sdn. Bhd. has grown from a local molding shop into a regional leader in precision engineering plastics. Strategically located in Johor Bahru, we serve as a vital link in the global manufacturing supply chain.

Our expertise lies in handling high-performance engineering resins (ABS, PC, POM, PBT, and more), ensuring that every component meets the stringent durability and aesthetic requirements of the modern industrial world.

Why Partner With Us?

  • Decades of Experience: 35+ years of specialized molding knowledge.
  • Precision Guaranteed: Advanced machinery capable of ultra-tight tolerances.
  • End-to-End Service: From initial prototyping to mass production and logistics.
  • Quality Standards: Rigorous ISO-compliant inspection at every production stage.
