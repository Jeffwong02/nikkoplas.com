About Us

Decades of Manufacturing Excellence

Established in 1989, Industri Nikkoplas Sdn. Bhd. is a premier provider of plastic injection molding in Johor. With over 35 years of history, we maintain a legacy of reliability and precision.

Our commitment to quality is proven by our dual ISO 9001:2015 and ISO 14001:2015 certifications. We have proudly served our primary industrial partners for over 20 years, delivering consistent value through 5S standardized manufacturing.
