About Industri Nikkoplas

A Legacy of Precision in Johor Bahru

Established as a cornerstone of the Tampoi Industrial Estate, Industri Nikkoplas Sdn. Bhd. has spent decades refining the art of plastic injection molding. We have evolved from a local manufacturer into a critical link in the global supply chain for the electronics, telecommunications, and mechanical engineering sectors.

Our philosophy is built on the Zero-Defect principle. By integrating advanced molding technology with a highly skilled Malaysian workforce, we deliver components that meet the rigorous standards of multinational corporations across Asia and Europe.

Strategically located near the Johor-Singapore Causeway, we offer unparalleled logistical advantages, ensuring that high-precision parts reach our clients with minimal lead times and maximum reliability.

Core Competencies

  • High-Tolerance Molding: Specializing in complex geometries and thin-wall components.
  • Advanced Materials: Expertise in engineering plastics (ABS, PC, PBT, PPS).
  • Tooling Maintenance: In-house mold servicing to ensure production consistency.
  • Quality Assurance: Equipped with coordinate measuring machines (CMM) for verification.
