About Industri Nikkoplas

35 Years of Engineering Heritage

Founded in 1989, Industri Nikkoplas Sdn. Bhd. is a premier one-stop service provider for precision plastics. Our manufacturing culture is built on rigorous 5S methodology, ensuring a safe, organized, and highly efficient production floor.

With around 80 skilled professionals and 35 heavy industrial machines in the Tampoi Industrial Area, we offer end-to-end solutions from tooling to final assembly for the global electronics and telecom sectors.
